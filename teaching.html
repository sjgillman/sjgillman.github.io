Teaching & Outreach

Principles of Ecology Lab: BI210L

As a teaching assistant for the Principles of Ecology lab, I had the opportunity to introduce undergraduate students to a variety of concepts including ecological succession, conducting laboratory and field research, scientific reading & writing, as well as teaching students to identify the local flora and fauna. I was also involved in course development for each of the labs.
